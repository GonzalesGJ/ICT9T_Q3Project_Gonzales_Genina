Lea Salonga Biography

Birth Name:
Maria Lea Carmen Imutan Salonga

Birth Place:
Manila, Philippines

Profession:
Actor, Singer

FUN FACTS


Originated the role of Kim in the 1989 West End production of Miss Saigon; she later transferred with the show to Broadway in 1991
Named as one of the 50 Most Beautiful People in the World by People magazine in 1991
Provided the singing voices for Princess Jasmine from Aladdin (1992) and Fa Mulan from Mulan (1998), and later reprised the role in other Disney productions
Became the first Asian actress to play the role of Éponine in the musical Les Misérables on Broadway in 1993
Was the first Filipino artist to be signed to an international record label with Atlantic Records in 1993
Was appointed the Goodwill Ambassador for Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations in 2010
Was named a Disney Legend in 2011
